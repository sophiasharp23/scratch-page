I started playing tennis in my sophomore year of high school, and what began as a new challenge quickly grew into a passion. At first, I was a beginner, but I dedicated myself to improving, and with hard work and persistence, I made my way onto the high school team. Over the years, I learned a ton about the gameâ€”its strategies, techniques, and the mental focus required to succeed. Now, tennis is more than just a sport to me; it's something I truly love, and it's become a big part of who I am. The progress I've made and the joy I get from playing make it all worth it.
